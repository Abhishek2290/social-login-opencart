ONEALL Social Login for OpenCart 1.5.x & 2.x
---------------------------------------------
Give your customers ability to login with the desired social networks.


INSTALLATION
---------------------------------------------

1. Copy the files to the root of your shop

2. Go to https://app.oneall.com/signup/

3. Create free account at ONEALL.COM

3. Go to Extensions -> Modules -> ONEALL Social Login

4. Press "ONEALL Account Settings", enter info supplied from ONEALL.COM and press "Save"

WARNING: Always test every login method you are going to use. 
Most of them require some additional setup at ONEALL.COM
